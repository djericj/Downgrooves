Who we are

Behind the music...we are people.

Downgrooves is a collaboration music project of recording artists Mario & Eric J based out of Dallas, Texas. The duo has been producing music for over 20 years under the aliases Mario & Eric J and, now, Downgrooves. The pair appear on a variety of well-established and respected labels such as Soundscapes Digital, Source of Gravity, Armada, Incepto Music, Bonzai, Elliptical Sun Recordings, Mistique Music, OLD SQL Recordings, Balkan Connection, Emotional Content and many more.

Our music is available on iTunes and Beatport.

Downgrooves is available for original tracks and remixes. Please contact us using our contact form.
